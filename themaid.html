The Maid

by Nita Prose


Book Description:

A locked-room mystery and heart-warming novel, "The Maid" make a statement of all mysteries can be resolve through connection to the human heart.
Molly Gray is a twenty-five-year-old girl who has struggles in social skills and usually misintepret the communication of others toward her. Unfortunately, her grandmother, who help her the most passed away a few months ago. To overcome the intricacy of life, she immerses herself into working at a Grand Hotel as a hotel maid.
On one destined day, Molly's ordinary life udergo a profound transformation as she enters the room of the famous and wealthy Charles Black, who she found dead on his bed. Police claims that she is their leading suspect and now, Molly is in her quest alongsides with her long-lost friends to entangle the death of Mr. Black-to vindicate herself and to find the real killer.
