A Self-Teaching Guide: Chemistry

by Richard Post, Chad Snyder and Clifford Houk


Book Description:

"A Self-Teaching Guide: Chemistry" is a textbook that new learners can study with simple algebra.
Each chapter include an introductory statement, a list of objectives, the main teaching sessions and the tutorial material with topic-related exercises.
Learners can have the opportunity to learn new lessons, review accumulated knowledge and test their understanding by exercises included in the same chapter.
