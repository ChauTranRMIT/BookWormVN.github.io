Anne Frank: The Diary of A Young Girl

by Eleanor Roosevelt


Book Description:

The diary of Anne Frank has been found in the last several days of her living in the attic before she became one of the many victims in the Holocaust massacre.
In 1942, when the Nazis occupying Holland, a thirteen-year-old Anne Frank and her family were forcefully fled their home to Amsterdam and went into hiding, Two years later, their current living area has been betrayed by the Gestapo, they had to fled once again to lived in an old office building.
In her diary, Anne Frank vividly expressed the time that her family had to lived in boredom, hunger and fear of death. However, a brave, young girl demonstrated the hard life with surprising humor, impressive optimism and courage.
