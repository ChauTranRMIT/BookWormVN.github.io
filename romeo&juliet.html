Romeo and Juliet

by William Shakespeare


Book Description:

Romeo and Juliet came from two rival families of Montague and Capulet. As their love intensifies, they hope their union might bring an end to the family feud. However, fate has other plans for the young lovebirds. Tragic circumstances occur, and their love faces intensive opposition from their families, resulting in a series of miscommunications, misunderstandings, conflicts and the worst, casualties. The story is filled with emotions, passion, and conflicts between individual desires and social constraints.
William Shakespeare, masterfully implied the concept of love, desires and temporary social constraints throughout the play. "Romeo and Juliet" is a heart-trending tale of young love and the miserable consequences of hatred, reminding audiences heavily of the consequecnes of the grudge and fragility of young love.
