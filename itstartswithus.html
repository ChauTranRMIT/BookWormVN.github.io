It Starts With Us

by Colleen Hoover


Book Description:

The story starts when Lily and her ex-husband, Ryle, have just settled down for a civil co-parenting conversation for her daughter, when she suddenly bumps into her first love, Atlas, again. After two years of separation, she got heart-fluttering once again, as she immediately says yes when Atlas asks her on a date.
However, Lily is being caught up between two men. Ryle is no longer in her marriage but he is still a huge part of her daughter life, whereas Atlas is the one who will not feel entirely comfortable with his love-interest still hanging around with her ex-husband and daughter. The biggest question of the novel is how Lily will live with her decision and the battle between gut and love.
