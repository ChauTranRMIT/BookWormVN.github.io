The Case For Space

by Robert Zubrin (Author), Mike Lenz (Narrator), Rowman & Littlefield (Publisher)


Book Description:

The main principle of the book discuss how the revolution in spacelight opens up a future of potential and limitless possibility which can occur to our lives on Earth and further scientific studies of experts. Written by an astronautical engineer, Robert Zubrin introduces how he is seeing promising innovation that can results in space travel, space observatories or even travelling to the stars.
Above all, he makes a more compelling case for why we need to get certain things done in the right way after implementing several theories. Even though exploring the unreveal of the outer world is interesting, our prime mission is always to protect our Earth and ensure the well-being of humanity. The purpose of conducting scientific studies and researchs is to increase knowledge, safeguard our shelter from stray asteroids, expanding beyond our homebase and to protect humanity from false claim that there is not enough for all.
